Hornet

Hornet is a vessel-like creature that you encounter throughout the game and fight twice.

It is suggested that she is considered a sibling of the vessels, due to the title of the "Sealed Siblings" ending, as well as her appearance, which is similar to that of the vessels.

She was given to Herrah the Beast as a way to appease her and have her join the dreamers.

Hornet is promised to be the second playable character, arriving in 2018, with a new character taking a similar place in her route.

Hornet's weapon of choice is the needle and thread, which is presumably from the spiders that she grew up with.

Hollow Knight is a metroidvania style platformer developed by Team Cherry and released in February 2017.

It was originally funded on Kickstarter, with a goal of AU$35,000. People pledged AU$57,138. This allowed the crew to reach the stretch goal of a second playable character, Hornet, which is coming in 2018.

The game was developed on Unity and released for Mac, Linux, and Windows. They will be making a Switch version, as per the stretch goal.

The second playable character, Hornet, will be available in 2018. Leading up to her release, Team Cherry has released two free DLCs, Hidden Dreams and The Grimm Troupe, with a third to be announced and released in 2018.

Hidden Dreams and The Grimme Troupe had incrementally more information so the third DLC should hold some major answers and content.

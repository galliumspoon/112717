Grimm, Fancy Boi The Troupe Leader

Grimm is the main boss of the Grimm Troupe DLC.

As shown in his title card, Grimm is the leader of the Grimm Troupe. He evolves into the Nightmare King later in the game.

The Grimm Troupe is a circus like group of travelers that pass through old and dying civilizations to perform a ritual that replaces the old troupe leader with the Grimmkin, a small creature that evolves as you collect flames and is assumed to help preserve the heart.

Grimm gifts you with the pin that gives you the Grimmkin and allows you to see the keepers of the flame.

It is believed that Grimm is another entity similar to the Radiance after becoming the Nightmare King. This is because the dream essence tokens are shown but with a different color (indicating nightmares) and as the Nightmare King has the only full screen title card before the boss fight, besides the Radiance.
